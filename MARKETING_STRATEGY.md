# 🚀 Marketing Strategy: Authority Over Algorithms

**Objective:** Position Jeff Batton not just as a coach, but as the *category king* of "Love Wound" work.

## 1. The "Google Trap" (SEO vs. LLM Optimization)
You asked: *"Will Google love it?"*
**Yes.** I have optimized the site with:
*   **Meta Tags:** Clear descriptions for "Relationship Coaching" and "Love Wound".
*   **Sitemap:** A map for Google to crawl every page.
*   **Performance:** Fast load times (which Google loves).

**BUT... The Game Has Changed.**
In 2025, high-net-worth clients don't just "Google" for a therapist. They ask **AI** (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) or they trust **Authority**.

### The New SEO: "LLM Optimization"
To get recommended by AI, you need **Content Density**.
*   **The Book:** Having a book is the #1 signal to AI that you are an expert.
*   **The Framework:** Your unique terms ("The Mirror," "Love Wound") train the AI to associate *you* with these concepts.
*   **The Strategy:** We don't just want to rank for "relationship coach." We want to rank for **"What is the Love Wound?"**

## 2. The "Authority" Strategy (Better Than SEO)
Instead of fighting for generic keywords, we build a **Media Moat**.

### A. The "Podcast Tour" (High Leverage)
*   **Don't start a podcast.** *Guest* on them.
*   **Target:** Podcasts for Entrepreneurs, High-Performers, and "Biohackers of the Mind."
*   **The Pitch:** "Why High-Performers Fail at Love (The Love Wound)."
*   **Result:** One hour of talking = 10,000 qualified listeners.

### B. The "Affiliate Army" (Referrals)
*   **Therapists:** They are burned out. They *want* to refer the "difficult" high-performers to someone who can handle them.
*   **Divorce Attorneys:** The best source of clients in crisis.
*   **The Offer:** "Send me your toughest cases. I handle the strategy; you handle the legal/emotional support."

### C. The "LinkedIn Thought Leader"
*   **Platform:** LinkedIn is where the money is (Executives/Founders).
*   **Content:** Short, punchy posts about the intersection of *Business Success* and *Relationship Failure*.
*   **Hook:** "You run a $10M company but can't run a dinner conversation without a fight. Here is why."

1.  **Website:** It is technically perfect (SEO ready).
