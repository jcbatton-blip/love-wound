## Core Methodology
The Mirror Method™: A framework for identifying and breaking subconscious relationship patterns formed in early childhood. Based on the concept that relationship patterns aren't problems to fix, but maps back to the original "Love Wound Imprint" - the subconscious definition of how love works absorbed before language.

## The Six Love Wounds
1. **Abandonment Wound** - Fear of being left; leads to clinging, chasing, or choosing unavailable partners
2. **Rejection Wound** - Fear of not being enough; leads to dimming your light or performing for acceptance
3. **Betrayal Wound** - Fear of being deceived; leads to hypervigilance and withholding trust
4. **Invisibility Wound** - Fear of not mattering; leads to over-giving and disappearing in relationships
5. **Suffocation Wound** - Fear of losing autonomy; leads to pulling away and resisting commitment
6. **Inadequacy Wound** - Fear of not being enough; leads to overachieving and proving worth
