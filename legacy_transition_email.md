# Legacy Client Transition Strategy & Scripts

## The Strategy: "The Velvet Rope"
**Goal:** Move clients to the new model without losing them or damaging the relationship.
**Concept:** You are not "raising prices" on them. You are "grandfathering" them into a VIP tier that no longer exists for the public. This frames the transition as a benefit, not a penalty.

### Phase 1: The Announcement (Send Immediately)
**Subject:** A personal update (and a thank you)

Dear [Client Name],

I’m writing to share some big news about the evolution of my practice—and to thank you for being part of the journey that got me here.

As you know, the "Love Wound" work has been deepening. To support this, I’ve completely rebuilt my platform and methodology to focus on deeper, transformational "containers" rather than open-ended coaching.

**What this means for the public:**
Starting [Date], I am moving exclusively to a 3-month "Deep Dive" container model ($3,500) for all new clients. I will no longer be offering single sessions or hourly billing to the public.

**What this means for you:**
Because you’ve been with me before this shift, I want to honor our relationship.
**You are officially "Grandfathered."**

For the next **6 months**, you keep your current rate and arrangement exactly as it is. No changes. No price hikes. You have VIP access to the "Old World" pricing while I roll out the "New World" to everyone else.

You don't need to do anything right now. I just wanted you to know that while things are changing "out there," you are safe "in here."

Thank you for trusting me with your process.

Warmly,

Jeff

---

### Phase 2: The Transition (Send 1 Month Before Expiry)
**Subject:** The next step in our work

Hi [Client Name],

We’re coming up on the end of the 6-month "grandfather" period we discussed.

I’ve loved the work we’ve done. Looking ahead, I want to make sure you have the right level of support.

Since my practice has fully shifted to the "Love Wound Container" model, I’d love to invite you into that deeper format. It’s a more structured, high-touch way of working that I think you’d get a lot out of.

**Your Options:**
1.  **The Deep Dive Container:** We move into the new 3-month structure. (I can offer you a special "Alumni" rate of $X if you book by [Date]).
2.  **The Maintenance Tier:** If you feel complete with the heavy lifting, we can move to a monthly "check-in" model (1 call/month) at [Lower Price].
3.  **Completion:** If you feel you’ve got what you need for now, we can wrap up our formal engagement and you can always grab a "Mirror Session" ($350) whenever you need a tune-up.

Let me know what feels right for you. No pressure—just want to serve you best.

Best,

Jeff
