# 📊 Analytics Dashboard Guide

**Objective:** Stop guessing. Start tracking.

I have configured your website to track the metrics that actually matter for your business. You don't need to drown in data; you just need to watch these 3 numbers.

## 1. The "Money Metrics" (What We Are Tracking)
I have added custom code to track these specific actions:

| Event Name | What It Means | Why It Matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `booking_click` | Someone clicked "Apply" for Individual or Couples work. | This is your **Lead Volume**. |
| `affiliate_click` | Someone clicked "Get it on Amazon" for a book. | This is your **Passive Income** potential. |
| `checkout_start` | Someone clicked to buy "The Kit" or "Mirror Session". | This is your **Sales Pipeline**. |

## 2. How to Access Your Dashboard
Your analytics are powered by **Umami** (a privacy-focused alternative to Google Analytics).

1.  **Log In:** Go to your Manus Dashboard.
2.  **Click "Analytics":** You will see a clean, simple graph.
3.  **Filter by Events:**
    *   Click on the "Events" tab.
    *   You will see exactly how many people clicked "Individual" vs. "Couples".

## 3. How to Use This Data
*   **If `booking_click` is low:** You need more traffic (Podcast guesting).
*   **If `booking_click` is high but emails are low:** Your application form is too long.
*   **If `affiliate_click` is high:** Write more book reviews! Your audience trusts your taste.

## 4. Next Level: Google Search Console
To see *what people are typing* to find you:
1.  Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
2.  Add Property -> `https://jeffbatton.com`.
3.  This will tell you if people are searching for "Jeff Batton" (Brand) or "Relationship Coach" (Generic).

**Summary:**
You now have a "Cockpit" for your business. Check it once a week on Mondays. If the lines are going up, keep doing what you're doing.
